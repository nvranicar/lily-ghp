About Me

My name is Kelly Reynolds, interior designer, wife of 25 years and mother of 2 wonderful boys. I began my career in Cincinnati, Ohio after graduating from the University of Kentucky in Interior Design. There I worked for a commercial architectural, engineering and design firm. After a couple years working in design, I continued my education to receive a degree in telecommunications for television production. The plan was to utilize my design and production education to work in stage set design. Hollywood here I come! But as life goes, not always do plans come to fruition. I met my husband the second time around in college and my path took a different direction, which I’m thankful. For 25 plus years I have been involved with residential interior design, either by creating custom interiors to custom draperies and soft goods to running an antique booth.

These things were done as I stayed at home to raise my children. Now that they are almost grown I am concentrating on revving up my design career, so from this I began LI & LY Interiors (pronounced Lee & Lee). I have been blessed to have had the ability to design beautiful spaces for others. I’ve always felt that a well-designed space is a happy place. Regardless of budget, I love to achieve this by either using what you have or start from a blank canvas. I am dedicated to helping you create inspiring environments by meeting your needs and desires. With a fun, creative, personal approach your space will come to life! A well thought out happy space.
