FAQs

Is E-Design right for me?

E-Design is the perfect way to achieve a professionally designed space without consuming too much of your ​ time and budget. It is a way for you to implement a design suggestion at a do-it-yourself pace.

​Do you work with local clients in person?

Yes, if you are located in and near Lexington, KY, we would be glad to work with you in a more traditional capacity. We charge an hourly fee for local design services. Contact us and we will get back with you.

What payment methods do you accept?

Pay Pal is the method of payment we accept for those purchasing E-Design services.

Do you offer refunds?

The product we offer is a PDF file via e-mail, therefore there are no refunds.

How do I get in touch with you?

You may email us at any time and we will get back with you in a timely manner.
